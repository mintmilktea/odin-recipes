Bubble Tea

Description

A tasty and refreshing Asian drink consisting mainly of milk, tea, and chewy tapioca pearls.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • â…“ cup pearl tapioca
  • 1 cup brewed black tea
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to boil in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in 1 teaspoon of sugar until it dissolves.
  3. Put in the tapioca pearls.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Rinse, drain, and refrigerate until chilled.
  6. Pour tea, milk, and 4 teaspoons of sugar in a cocktail shaker.
  7. Stir until the sugar dissolves and the milk is well mixed.
  8. Add ice cubes and shake the whole drink.
  9. Pour the drink into a glass and add the tapioca pearls.
